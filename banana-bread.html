Banana Bread

A personal favorite, refined over many iterations.

Ingredients

Dry Ingredients

  • 250g All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Ground Cinnamon (optional)
  • 1/8 tsp Ground Nutmeg (optional)
  • 1 Cup chopped Walnuts, or chocolate chips, or both (all optional)

Wet Ingredients

  • 3 ripe Bananas, mashed
  • 2 large Eggs
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 3/4 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup Butter, softened

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Combine dry ingredients in a large bowl
  3. In a separate bowl, cream butter and sugar, then mix the wet ingredients until well combined
  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined
  5. Prepare your favorite loaf pan with a thin layer of butter, dusted with flour
  6. Transfer the batter into the prepared pan
  7. Bake for 55-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean
